____________
Transposition Cipher
____________

- Number of possible keys depends on the message's length
- Types:
        - Rail Fence Cipher
        - Route Cipher
        - Myszkowski Transposition Cipher
        - Disrupted Transposition Cipher
        - Columnar Transposition Cipher

- Transposition cipher rearranges the message symbols into an order that makes the original message unreadable.
- Each key creates a different ordering/permutation of the characters
